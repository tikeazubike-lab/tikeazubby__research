# 10 — Atomic Notes MOC

> **One idea per note.** The backbone of the Zettelkasten. Every permanent insight lives here.

---

## Purpose

Atomic notes are the smallest unit of knowledge. Each note contains exactly **one idea**, fully explained, with links to related ideas. They are evergreen — they don't become outdated because they capture principles, not events.

---

## Naming Convention

- **Format:** `Idea Title` (no dates — these are evergreen)
- **Examples:**
  - `Liquidity cascade effect`
  - `Funding rate divergence signals`
  - `BIM-to-AI pipeline concept`
  - `Email deliverability depends on reputation`

---

## Rules

1. **One idea per note** — if you need two ideas, make two notes
2. **Write in your own words** — never copy-paste
3. **Link to at least 2 other notes** — no orphans
4. **Add context** — why does this idea matter? Where did it come from?
5. **Use frontmatter** — always include YAML metadata
