# 90 — Archives MOC

> **Completed projects, inactive notes, old versions.** Out of sight, not deleted.

---

## What Goes Here

- Completed project notes (moved from [[40-Projects-MOC]])
- Superseded versions of notes
- Inactive domains or abandoned research
- Old journal entries (after yearly archive)

---

## Sub-Folders

- `90-Projects/Archived/` — completed projects
- `90-Atomic-Notes/Deprecated/` — superseded atomic notes
- `90-Literature/Processed/` — fully processed literature
- `90-Journal/Yearly/` — journal archives by year

---

## Archive Rules

1. **Never delete** — move to archive instead
2. **Add archive date** to frontmatter when moving
3. **Keep links intact** — archived notes still count as connections
4. **Review yearly** — anything inactive for 12+ months gets archived
