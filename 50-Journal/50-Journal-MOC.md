# 50 — Journal MOC

> **Daily reflections, trade reviews, and time-stamped logs.** The diary layer of the vault.

---

## Purpose

Journal notes capture what happened, what you felt, what you learned — in the moment. They are dated, personal, and raw. Over time, review them to extract patterns and insights into your atomic notes.

---

## Sub-Folders

- `50-Journal/Daily/` — daily notes (one per day)
- `50-Journal/Weekly/` — weekly reviews and reflections
- `50-Journal/Trade-Reviews/` — individual trade post-mortems
- `50-Journal/Monthly/` — monthly retrospectives

## Weekly Review Ritual

Every Sunday (or your chosen day):

1. **Review** — Read all daily notes from the week
2. **Extract** — Pull insights into atomic notes
3. **Connect** — Link new atomic notes to existing ones
4. **Plan** — Set focus for next week on [[00-Dashboard]]
5. **Clean** — Process any remaining inbox items
6. **AI Synthesis** — Run weekly AI review (see [[60-AI-MOC]])
