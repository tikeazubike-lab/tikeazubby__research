## Learning Principles

1. **Just-in-time learning** — learn what you need for the current project
2. **Build to learn** — projects cement knowledge better than courses
3. **Teach to retain** — write atomic notes as if explaining to someone else
4. **Spaced repetition** — revisit notes at increasing intervals
